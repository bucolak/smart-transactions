Privacy Policy

How {{ config.APP_NAME }} protects your data

We respect your privacy and design our platform to keep tenant data isolated, encrypted, and auditable. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and how you can exercise your rights.

1. Data we collect
  • Account data: names, emails, role assignments, organization details.
  • Product data: projects, invoices, subscription usage, AI interaction logs.
  • Security data: login timestamps, IP addresses for security events, audit trails.
  • Technical data: browser user agents, device metadata, and cookie identifiers for session management.
2. How we use data

We use your data to provide and improve the Service: authenticate users, enforce tenant isolation, deliver notifications, process payments, provide support, and monitor platform health. AI features are bound to your tenant and logged for auditing.

3. Storage and security

We use SQLite by default with strict foreign key enforcement and per-tenant scoping. Data is stored in the instance directory by default. We enforce HTTPOnly cookies, TLS-first links, least-privilege roles, and audit logging. Attachments such as logos are stored in dedicated upload folders scoped by organization.

4. Third-party services

We may use third-party processors to deliver parts of the Service, such as:

  • Payments via Stripe or Razorpay.
  • Email delivery via SendGrid or configured SMTP providers.
  • Optional AI models (e.g., Gemini) when explicitly invoked within your tenant context.

These providers process data on our behalf under appropriate agreements.

5. Cookies and tracking

We use strictly necessary cookies for authentication, session continuity, and CSRF safety where enabled. We do not use advertising trackers. Cookies are set with HttpOnly and SameSite attributes; Secure is enabled in production.

6. Data retention

We retain data for as long as your organization maintains an account or as required to provide the Service. You may request deletion of certain data, subject to legal and operational constraints. Backups and logs may persist for a limited period for security and compliance.

7. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. Contact us using the Support page to submit a request. We will respond in accordance with applicable law.

8. International transfers

Data may be processed in regions where our infrastructure or providers operate. We implement safeguards appropriate to the transfer and comply with applicable cross-border data rules.

9. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10. Changes

We may update this policy to reflect product or legal changes. Material updates will be communicated. Continued use of the Service after updates indicates acceptance.

11. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, use our Support page or email founders@smart-transactions.app. We respond with tenant-aware context to reduce friction for your security team.
